# Campaigns

Generate multiple creatives in a single operation — targeted by audience profile, across multiple formats, with a shared brief.

---

## Create a campaign

A campaign is created in **3 steps**.

---

### Step 1 — Images

**Reference image** *(optional)*
An existing visual the AI uses for style and composition inspiration. Can be a competitor ad, a brand photo, or a moodboard.

**Product image** *(optional)*
The product to feature. The AI integrates it into the generated scene.

Accepted formats: PNG, JPG. Import from your computer, your library, or a template.

---

### Step 2 — Targeting

**Audience profiles**
Select up to 12 profiles. Each profile generates creatives tailored to its persona, pain point, and message angle.

> No profiles yet? → [Generate profiles](./angles.md)

**CTA text**
The action button on the ad. E.g.: `Discover`, `Order`, `Shop now`.

**Offer text**
The main promotion or headline. E.g.: `-25% this weekend`, `Free delivery from €35`.

**Brand Kit**
Enable the toggle to automatically apply your colors and visual identity.

---

### Step 3 — Settings

**Volume**
Total number of creatives to generate (1–60). Volume is distributed across the selected profiles and formats.

Example: 3 profiles × 2 formats = 6 slots. Volume 12 → 2 creatives per slot.

**Formats**
Check one or more formats:
- `1:1` — Square Feed
- `4:5` — Portrait Feed
- `9:16` — Stories & Reels
- `1.91:1` — Landscape

**Language**
Language of the text generated on creatives: French, English, Spanish, etc.

**Context document** *(optional)*
Upload a brief, a PDF, or a product page URL to enrich the generation context.

---

## Launch generation

Click **Generate**. Progress is shown in real time.

Estimated time: **2–5 minutes** depending on volume (fal.ai queue).

---

## Manage creatives

Once generation is complete, your creatives appear in the campaign gallery.

**Available views**
- Grid — quick visual preview
- List — details (profile, format, score, date)

**Filters**
- By audience profile
- By format

**Actions on a creative**
- Download (PNG)
- Create a variation (same style, new content)
- Reformat to a different ratio
- View the tree (variants and iterations)
- Export as ZIP (multi-select)

**Creative score**
Each visual receives a score based on visual appeal and alignment with the profile. Use it to prioritize your A/B tests.

---

## Volume strategy

| Goal | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Quick test | 1 profile × 1 format × volume 1 |
| A/B testing | 2 profiles × 2 formats × volume 2 = 8 creatives |
| Full campaign | 3–5 profiles × 3 formats × volume 3 = 27–45 creatives |
| Multi-client agency | Weekly script via CLI or SDK |
