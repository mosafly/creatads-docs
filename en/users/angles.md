# Creative Angles

**Angles** are the core unit in CreatAds. They replace the old "audience profile" concept to align with how Meta Andromeda (Advantage+) actually works: targeting is no longer the lever — creative diversity is. Your job is no longer to define 10 demographic personas, but to feed the algorithm **10 distinct creative angles** so it can learn, format by format, what converts.

---

## What an angle contains

A creative angle has four dimensions:

| Field | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Name** | Short internal label (5 words max) | "Pain – morning dark circles" |
| **Hook** | The opening punchline (8–15 words) | "Your dark circles betray you in the first meeting of the day." |
| **Awareness level** | Where the audience stands | `unaware` / `problem_aware` / `solution_aware` / `product_aware` / `most_aware` |
| **Visual style** | The creative's look | `ugc` / `studio` / `lifestyle` / `text_heavy` / `before_after` / `demo` |
| **Copy direction** | Tone + key benefit + CTA suggestion | "Confident, urgent. Lead with -30% offer. CTA: Shop now." |

The AI injects these four dimensions into both the image prompt and the generated copy — so each angle yields a visually and message-distinct creative.

---

## Why this structure

**Awareness level** (Eugene Schwartz) — determines who the ad speaks to:
- `unaware` → curiosity, problem reveal
- `problem_aware` → press on the pain
- `solution_aware` → comparison, why-us
- `product_aware` → offer, urgency
- `most_aware` → social proof, FOMO, loyalty

**Visual style** — determines the format. Meta favors accounts that mix UGC, studio, lifestyle and demo rather than a single repeated look.

**Hook** — the promise in the first second. Without a hook, the algorithm can't optimize CTR.

**Copy direction** — guides headline, body, and description generation during campaign preparation.

## Generate 10 angles with AI

Recommended method: let the AI generate **10 diversified angles** from your brand description.

1. Go to **Brand Setup → Creative angles**
2. Fill the research summary panel
3. Click **Generate**
4. Select the output language

The AI produces **10 angles distributed under an Andromeda-native rule**:
- 2× unaware
- 3× problem_aware
- 2× solution_aware
- 2× product_aware
- 1× most_aware

With at least **4 different visual styles** out of the 6 available.

**Example effective description:**
```
French artisan chocolate brand, premium positioning, gift market,
30-55 year olds looking for quality gifts. Differentiator: bean-to-bar,
unique origins, Parisian workshops.
```

Generation in **30–60 seconds**, immediately usable in your campaigns.

---

## Create an angle manually

If you have a clear idea:

1. In **Brand Setup → Angles**, click **Add**
2. Fill the 5 fields (name, hook, awareness level, visual style, copy direction)
3. Save

The angle appears in the list, immediately usable.

---

## Use angles in a campaign

In the campaign wizard (Step 2), select up to 12 angles. Each angle:
- generates its own visuals (based on its visual style)
- gets its own copy (based on its copy direction)
- creates its own adset on Meta Ads (based on its awareness level → top/mid/bottom funnel)

→ See [Campaigns](./campaigns.md)

---

## Manage your angles

Angles are linked to the active client workspace. You can:
- View full details (name, hook, awareness level, visual style, copy direction)
- Filter existing creatives by angle
- Regenerate the pool of 10 angles when your positioning evolves

> Angles are shared across all your campaigns for that client. If your offer changes, regenerate to get a new aligned pool.
