# Audience Profiles

Profiles are AI personas that represent your target segments. Each profile steers generation toward a message and visual style adapted to that audience.

---

## What a profile contains

A profile has three elements:

| Field | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Persona** | Who this person is | "Woman 30-45, upper-income, urban, values authenticity" |
| **Pain point** | Their main frustration or barrier | "Fear of giving an impersonal or disappointing gift" |
| **Angle** | The message axis to use | "'Transmission' approach: highlight artisan know-how as proof of value" |

The AI uses these three elements to adapt composition, dominant colors, and generated text.

---

## Generate profiles

The fastest method: let the AI generate **10 profiles** from a description of your brand.

1. Go to **Brand Setup → Profiles**
2. Click **Generate profiles**
3. Enter a description of your brand (the richer, the better the profiles)
4. Select the output language

**Example of an effective description:**
```
French artisan chocolate brand, premium positioning,
gift market, targeting 30-55 year olds looking for quality gifts
for special occasions. Differentiator:
bean-to-bar, unique origins, Parisian workshops.
```

Generation takes **30–60 seconds** and produces 10 distinct profiles, immediately available in your campaigns.

---

## Choosing the right profiles

After generation, read each profile and note the 2–3 most relevant for your next campaign.

**Selection criteria:**
- The persona matches your primary target
- The pain point reflects a real purchase barrier
- The angle is consistent with your current commercial message

---

## Using profiles in a campaign

In the campaign creation wizard (Step 2), select up to 12 profiles. Each profile generates its own creatives — giving you multiple creative angles in a single batch.

→ See [Campaigns](./campaigns.md)

---

## Managing your profiles

Profiles are linked to the active client workspace. You can:
- View full details (persona, pain point, angle)
- Filter your existing creatives by profile
- Add profiles manually if you already have defined personas

> Profiles are shared across all your campaigns for that client. If your targets change, regenerate to get updated profiles.
